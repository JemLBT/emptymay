BACKGROUND

In 2025, I completed a web design course and qualified as a junior developer. Around the same time, AI was rapidly evolving, and opportunities for junior creatives were becoming more tricky to find.

Writing and storytelling had always been a huge part of my life, so I decided to combine both passions and create this interactive fiction experience!

Every choice shapes the story. I hope you enjoy whichever path you choose.
